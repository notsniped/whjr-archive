How to Play


NOTE: You can only play this game on Windows/MacOS/Linux. No support is provided for iOS/iPadOS/Android/other OSes.

In this game you compete against a JavaScript AI in a game of Ping-Pong. To move the paddle up, simply move your cursor upwards. To move your paddle down, move your corsor down.
You will get 6 lives. If you die 6 times while playing, you completely lose the game. But if you want to you can also restart it.

The game in itself is very self explainatory, you should be able to play it with ease. Enjoy!
